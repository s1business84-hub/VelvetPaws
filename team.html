Co-Founder & CEO

Sophia Al-Mansoori

Sophia has loved animals her entire life. With a background in luxury retail management and a BSc in Business Administration from UOWD, she combined her passion and expertise to launch Velvet Paws in 2019.

"I wanted to create a space where pet owners didn't have to compromise — where quality, care, and elegance all came together under one roof."

— Sophia Al-Mansoori, Founder

Co-Founder & Head of Products

Khalid Al-Rashid

Khalid brings 12 years of luxury goods procurement experience to Velvet Paws. He is the curator behind our world-class product selection, personally vetting every brand and supplier for quality and ethical standards.
