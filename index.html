Our Story

Crafted for Pets
Who Deserve the Best

Born from a deep love of animals, Velvet Paws was founded in 2019 with a singular vision — to bring luxury, warmth, and expert care to every pet in Dubai. We believe your pet isn't just an animal; they're family.

From hand-stitched leather collars to aromatherapy baths, every product and service at Velvet Paws is thoughtfully curated to ensure the happiest, healthiest life for your companion.
