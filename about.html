Our Story

From a small dream to Dubai's most beloved pet boutique — the Velvet Paws journey.

Who We Are

Born from Love,
Built with Purpose

Velvet Paws was founded in 2019 by Sophia Al-Mansoori, a lifelong animal lover who felt that Dubai's pet care scene lacked the warmth, expertise, and luxury that pets — and their owners — truly deserved.

What started as a small grooming studio in Dubai Marina quickly grew into a full luxury pet boutique, thanks to an incredible team and a community of devoted pet parents who trusted us with their most precious family members.

Today, Velvet Paws is more than a pet shop. It is a sanctuary — a place where every wagging tail and gentle purr is celebrated.
